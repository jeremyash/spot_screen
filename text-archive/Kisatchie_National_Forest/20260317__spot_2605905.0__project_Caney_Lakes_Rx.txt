.DISCUSSION...

No morning fog today, but high clouds will start to move into the
region during the late morning hours and remain through the
afternoon and evening. Temperatures will start off below freezing, 
but we should see warmer afternoon temperatures, as highs will 
climb into the mid to upper 50s. Mixing heights should climb 
around 4000 ft, but a dry airmass will keep RH values down in the 
low to mid 20s. The 20 winds will be much lighter today, starting 
off from calm to light & variable through late morning, before 
southerly winds between 5-10 mph return by this afternoon. 
Although we will not issue a Fire Danger Statement today due to 
the lighter winds, the low RH values will still be a concern for 
wildfire growth.

With the return of southerly winds, expect a warming trend on
Wednesday along with increased afternoon RH values. Mixing 
heights will likely climb to near 4000 ft again.
